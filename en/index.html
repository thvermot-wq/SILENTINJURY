Bio

Since 2021, Silent Injury has delivered direct metalcore: a brutal rhythm foundation, slicing lead guitar, swelling bass, and vocals shifting between screams and clean lines. The band brings a stage-calibrated repertoire with a modern sound and razor-sharp execution.

Bombs outside, fake paradise inside: cracks are everywhere.

Visualizer — Parallel Heights

Lyrics / mood: Parallel Heights

“Parallel Heights” embodies the voice of a man entrenched at the very top of his tower of glass and screens, replacing people with curves. From the first line, he states it brutally: “They’re 99 / I’m one of the first.” Down below, people crawl in the street; up above, he stacks “confcall with lawyers” to build a “parallel reality.” Nasdaq is no longer an index—it is a god—and the confession drops with the mask: “I don’t like people / Only numbers.” When the bridge arrives — “Send him to stars… / Aliens won’t thank us” — the varnish cracks completely: you hear the hatred and, behind it, the panic that one day someone down there may find the door.
